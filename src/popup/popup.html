Dynamic Captions

Configuration

Adjusts how smoothly the captions follow the speaker's face. Higher values feel smoother by ignoring small movements, while lower values offer strict and snappy alignment but use more CPU.

0.4

How strict the AI is when identifying a face. Make this stricter if the captions are tracking non-faces, or loosen it if it frequently loses track of the actual speaker.

3

How long to wait after the speaker leaves the frame before dropping the captions back to the bottom of the screen.
